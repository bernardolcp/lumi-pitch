**On screen:** Photo of Maria + "Insurance broker. São Paulo."

**Speech:**
Maria is an insurance broker in São Paulo. Single mom, 13-year-old son. She wakes up at 6, makes breakfast, drops him at school, and by 8 she's at her desk — quoting, calling, following up. She's good. Really good. Built her book of business door by door, coffee by coffee.

Last Tuesday, 11pm, she's helping her son with homework. Her phone buzzes. A client wants a quote for rental insurance. She thinks — I'll get to it first thing tomorrow. By 7am, she opens the message. He already signed with someone else.

That happens every week. Not because she's not good enough. Because she's only one person.

Now imagine Maria has Lumi. That same Tuesday, 11:14pm, the client messages. An AI specialist responds in 30 seconds. Sends a personalized quote. Explains the coverage. The client signs before Maria wakes up. She opens her phone at 7am — but this time, to a notification: "New policy sold."

**On screen:** HTML animation (engine-animation.html) — 5 steps appearing sequentially, "Marketing Agency" and "Sales Team" crossed out, feedback loop.

**Speech:**
It starts with research — scanning trends, mapping competitors, understanding what her customers actually say online. Then it creates — fifty ad creatives a day, posted across every channel. It optimizes — reallocating budget between Meta, Google, and TikTok in real time, A/B testing everything.
